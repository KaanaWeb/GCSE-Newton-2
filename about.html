Our Vision

Education as Ultimate Liberation

Balu believes in education to be the ultimate liberation for every human. No child will be left behind. We at GCSE NEWTON focus on different learning capabilities of children. Every child is different in their intellectual capacities. And that's natural and completely alright.

Which is a strength rather than a weakness. Don't give a second thought if we could help you or otherwise. Visit us today. No need to take any appointment. Just walk-in, and we are ready to talk to you.

Our Mission

Unlocking Intellectual Beauty

Mind is beautiful, and we at GCSE NEWTON believe in the capability of that mind to conquer fear, anxiety and doubt towards learning something new. We have created this platform to unlock the intellectual beauty in every child.

We are instilling confidence in every child. We handhold while they walk towards success. Within a week of teaching, you will observe a substantial difference in your child's energy, enthusiasm, and seriousness towards studies.

They may also teach you back what they learn from us. And we encourage that attitude, as learning is enhanced through knowledge-sharing.
