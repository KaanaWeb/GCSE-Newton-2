FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and teaching methods.

Our classes are structured to include a mix of theory, practical examples, and interactive exercises. We focus on building a strong foundation of knowledge and then applying it to real-world problems and exam-style questions. Each session includes time for questions and personalized support.

We maintain small class sizes with a maximum student-to-teacher ratio of 8:1. This ensures that each student receives personalized attention and support. For students who need additional help, we also offer one-to-one sessions.

We use a combination of regular assessments, practice exams, and continuous feedback to track student progress. Parents receive detailed progress reports, and we schedule regular meetings to discuss achievements and areas for improvement. Our goal is to ensure that both students and parents are fully informed about academic development.

We offer additional support sessions for students who need extra help in specific subjects. These can be arranged as one-to-one sessions or small group tutorials focused on particular topics or skills. Our teachers are always available to provide guidance and support outside of regular class hours.

Enrolling your child is simple. You can visit our center, call us, or fill out the enrollment form on our website. We'll then schedule an initial assessment to understand your child's current level and learning needs. This helps us create a personalized learning plan and place them in the appropriate class.
